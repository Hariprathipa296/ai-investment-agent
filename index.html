📚 Learn Investment

📈 Stocks

Stocks represent ownership in a company where you buy a small share of that business. If the company performs well, the value of your stock increases.

📊 Mutual Funds

Mutual funds pool money from multiple investors managed by financial experts. This helps reduce risk compared to investing in a single stock.

📉 ETFs

Exchange Traded Funds are a collection of multiple stocks combined into one investment. They allow you to invest in many companies at once.

⚠️ Risk

Low-risk investments provide stable but lower returns over time. High-risk investments can give higher profits but also bigger losses.
